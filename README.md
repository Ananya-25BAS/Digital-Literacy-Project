Overview of the Project-
This project focused on building digital literacy and professional online skills through five structured tasks. Each module introduced practical aspects such as creating infographics, setting up a digital portfolio, exploring coding and collaboration platforms, drafting professional emails with etiquette, and researching cybercrime awareness. Together, these tasks helped strengthen technical abilities, communication skills, and responsible online behaviour.
Summary-
Through this project, I gained a deeper understanding of digital literacy, professional communication, collaboration tools, and cybercrime awareness. Each task—from designing infographics and building a digital portfolio to exploring coding platforms, drafting professional emails, and researching cybercrime—helped me connect theory with practical skills. I learned how important it is to maintain a professional online presence, practice safe internet habits, and use digital tools responsibly. The case study on phishing especially highlighted the risks of careless online behaviour and the need for vigilance. Overall, this project has not only improved my technical and communication skills but also shaped my digital identity as a student. Moving forward, I will apply these lessons to academic work, internships, and professional networking, ensuring that I remain smart, safe, and responsible in the digital world.
